Our Story

Brake Knights was founded by a Northern Virginia mechanic who got tired of watching customers wait hours at traditional shops for repairs that could be done on-site. Armed with years of ASE certification and a fully equipped service vehicle, the first Brake Knight hit the road — and hasn't stopped since.

Today, we've completed over 5,000 brake repairs across Loudoun, Fairfax, Prince William, Arlington, and Alexandria counties. Every job is done with the same care and precision as the first.
