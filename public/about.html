Our Origin Story

A Knights Tale

In a land filled with the clatter of wrenches and the scent of motor oil, a young squire named Alex James set forth on his noble quest. Trained in the ancient arts of automotive repair, he honed his skills in the grand halls of trade schools and the forges of dealership service bays. He wielded his tools with precision, gaining the wisdom of the realm's greatest mechanics. But as he journeyed through the kingdom of auto repair, he uncovered a dark truth: hidden fees, deceptive upsells, and weary travelers trapped in the waiting rooms of despair.

It was an injustice too great to ignore.

With steely resolve and an ASE-certified blade (er, wrench), Sir Alex took up arms against these foul practices. Over the years, he has battled brake failures on over 5,000 chariots, restoring them to their full stopping power and ensuring the safety of their noble riders. Yet, the realm still needed more: a true order of knights, sworn to integrity, honesty, and quality. Thus, the banner of Brake Knights was raised, a company built on the code of honor, standing as a beacon for all in need of brake repair without the hassle of the dreaded repair shop.

Gone are the days of long waits and needless expenses. Brake Knights ride to you, armed with expertise and a promise: No deception. No fluff. Just honest, high-quality brake service, wherever you are.

So, weary traveler, when your chariot falters and your steed's brakes cry for aid, fear not! Summon Brake Knights, and we shall answer the call.
