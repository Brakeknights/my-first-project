Brake Pad & Rotor Replacement

The most frequently needed brake service, and one of the most critical for your safety. We replace worn pads and damaged rotors at your location, restoring full stopping power.

Call Request Service

What Is Brake Pad & Rotor Replacement?

Your brake pads press against the rotors to slow the vehicle. Over time, pads wear thin and rotors develop grooves, warping, or excessive wear. When either component degrades beyond safe limits, replacement is required, and we always recommend replacing pads and rotors as a set for maximum performance and warranty coverage.

Signs You Need This Service

  • Squealing or grinding noise when braking
  • Brake pedal feels soft or spongy
  • Vehicle takes longer to stop than usual
  • Vibration or shuddering when applying brakes
  • Visible wear indicator on pad (less than ¼ inch remaining)

Our On-Site Process

We arrive at your location fully equipped. Our technician removes each wheel, inspects the entire brake assembly, installs new pads and rotors, lubricates caliper slide pins, and road-tests the vehicle before completing the job.

Typical Time & Pricing

One axle takes approximately 1–1.5 hours. Two axles take 2–2.5 hours. Pricing starts at $525 per axle, including parts and labor, significantly less than most dealerships or shops.
