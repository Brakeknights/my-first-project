Professional Brake Inspection & Diagnosis

Not sure if your brakes need work? Our $60 comprehensive inspection gives you a complete picture — pads, rotors, calipers, hoses, and fluid — with an honest, written report and zero pressure.

Book Inspection ($60) 📞 Call 703-977-4475

What Our Inspection Covers

  • Brake pad thickness measurement (all four wheels)
  • Rotor thickness and surface condition
  • Caliper function and slide pin movement
  • Brake hose integrity (cracking, bulging, leaks)
  • Brake fluid condition and moisture content
  • Parking brake operation
  • Brake warning light diagnosis

What You Get

After the inspection, you receive a clear verbal and written summary of findings — what's good, what's marginal, and what needs immediate attention. We give you our honest recommendation with no pressure to book additional services on the spot.

Who Should Get an Inspection?

  • Anyone hearing unusual noises when braking
  • Vehicles with unknown brake service history
  • Before buying a used vehicle
  • When the brake warning light comes on
  • If you notice any change in braking feel
  • Annual safety check for peace of mind

Time & Cost

A full brake inspection takes approximately 30–45 minutes. The fee is $60. If you proceed with any recommended repairs the same day, the inspection fee may be applied toward the repair cost.
