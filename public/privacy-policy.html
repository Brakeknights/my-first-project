Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Introduction

Brake Knights ("we," "us," or "our") operates brakeknights.com and provides mobile brake repair services in Northern Virginia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, phone number, email address, vehicle information, service address, and messages submitted through our contact form. We may also collect basic analytics data (page views, browser type) through standard web analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: respond to service inquiries and schedule appointments; send appointment confirmations and service updates via SMS or email; communicate about your brake repair service; improve our website and services. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

SMS Messaging Consent

By submitting our contact form, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Brake Knights at the phone number you provide. These messages may include appointment confirmations, service updates, and follow-up communications. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Message frequency varies.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, though some features may not function properly.

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (e.g., Facebook). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at greetings@brakeknights.com.

Contact Us
